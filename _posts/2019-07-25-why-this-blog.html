Why this blog?

This blog is dedicated to my own research on programming-related subjects. Whenever I need to search extensively to get an answer about something I will put what I have found and the resources that helped here as an archive. It is also intended for me to be able to keep a track of my own research and put them back into memory if needed.

If it can also be useful to others it will be more than I expected and will make my day. Also if you notice any error, want to ask a question or just want to say hello don't hesitate to contact me by mail or in the comments section below each post.

Thank you for reading this first post, see you in anothers one.
